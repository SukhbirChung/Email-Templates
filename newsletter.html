Shocking study finds:
Decline in Wand-Waving Skills at Hogwarts

A study at Hogwarts reveals a decline in wand-waving skills, with 65% of students struggling with basic charms. Reliance on enchanted devices and apps is believed to be the cause.

In response, Headmistress McGonagall plans to introduce mandatory wand-waving workshops and a curriculum focusing on spell-casting fundamentals. The goal is to restore traditional magic skills and maintain Hogwarts' reputation for excellence.
